Planning Your Visit

Here's what to expect when you stop by.

Open Hours

Non-members can visit on Saturdays from 2pm to midnight. Weekday visits available by appointment.

Free Parking

We have plenty of free parking available right outside our building. Look for the SBHX sign.

Safety First

All visitors must sign a liability waiver. Closed-toe shoes required in workshop areas.
