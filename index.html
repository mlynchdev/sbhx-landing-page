Build Anything

From traditional craftsmanship to cutting-edge fabrication, we've got the tools and space for your projects.

Woodworking

Craft beautiful projects with professional-grade tools.

  • Table saw & band saw
  • Planer & jointer
  • CNC router
  • Dust collection system

Metalworking

Shape and fabricate metal with precision equipment.

  • Mill & lathe
  • MIG & TIG welders
  • Grinders & sanders
  • Plasma cutter

Digital Fabrication

Precision cutting and engraving with computer control.

  • Laser cutters
  • CNC machines
  • Vinyl cutter
  • PCB mill

3D Printing

Turn digital designs into physical objects layer by layer.

  • FDM printers
  • Resin (SLA) printers
  • Large format builds
  • Multiple materials

Why Join SBHX?

More than just tools—we're a community of makers, learners, and innovators.

24/7 Access

Work on your projects whenever inspiration strikes. Members get round-the-clock access to the space.

Community

Connect with fellow makers, share knowledge, and collaborate on projects with diverse expertise.

Learn & Grow

Attend workshops, take classes, and learn to use new tools with guidance from experienced makers.

Storage Space

Keep your ongoing projects safe with dedicated storage options for members.

Pro Equipment

Access tools that would cost tens of thousands to own—laser cutters, CNC, welders, and more.

Expert Help

Get assistance from knowledgeable members who can help troubleshoot and advise on your builds.
